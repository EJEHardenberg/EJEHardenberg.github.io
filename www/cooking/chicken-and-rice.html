Chicken and Rice

Difficulty: Medium

30 minutes Chicken and Spiced Rice

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup Rice
  • 1 cup water
  • Chili and Cumin Spices
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Red and Green Pepper
  • 2 Chicken breasts
Directions
  1. Boil water in a small pot
  2. Slice chicken into strips
  3. Chop Onion and Peppers
  4. Heat pan to medium heat and dump vegis in
  5. Add spices to boiling water (to taste)
  6. When water is boiled, add rice, turn heat to simmer low and add rice. Cover and don't open for 20 minutes
  7. When rice has been in for about 5-10 minutes, add chicken to vegitables
  8. turn chicken occasionally until rice is done
  9. Pour rice onto plate, pour vegitables on top and add soy sauce
  10. Eat.
