Potato Pancakes

Difficulty: medium

Tasty potato pancakes, serve it with applesauce or sugar!

Ingredients
  • 6 large potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • salt & pepper
  • spices to your liking
  • bread crumbs
  • Butter
Directions
  1. clean and peel the potatoes
  2. cut or process potatoes in a food processor (chunky or smooth, however you like em)
  3. break eggs into a bowl and season with Salt, pepper, and spices
  4. mix potatoes and egg mix together, add breadcrumbs as well.
  5. Let mixture sit for 5 minutes or so
  6. turn on a frying pan to medium heat and melt butter in the pan
  7. pour a bit of a mixture out into a pancake, flatten it about 1/4 inch and let fry
  8. once each side is golden brown flip it and brown the other side.
  9. Serve with sugar, cream, applesauce, or salt!
