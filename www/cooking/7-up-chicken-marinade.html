7-up Chicken Marinade

Difficulty: Easy

Delicious Marinade, really! Try it!

Ingredients
  • 2 Cups 7 up
  • 1 Cup Soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon horse radish
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 Cup Oil
Directions
  1. Mix all the ingredients in a container that will fit them all
  2. Cut chicken into strips and puncture with fork
  3. place chicken into marinade
  4. let sit for 8 hours in fridge
  5. Cook up your chicken and eat!
