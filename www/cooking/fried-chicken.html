Fried Chicken

Difficulty: Easy

Easy to make fried Chicken

Ingredients
  • chicken
  • breadcrumbs
  • oil
  • 1 egg
Directions
  1. Turn a frying pan to medium heat and pour enough oil to cover the bottom of the pan in.
  2. While you're waiting for the oil to heat up, pour the breadcrumbs into a bowl and beat an egg in other.
  3. Dip the chicken in the egg and then roll it into the breakcrumbs.
  4. Put each piece of breaded chicken aside until the oil in the pan is hot.
  5. Lay the chicken into the oil and turn each piece over every few minutes until the chicken is cooked well.
  6. Serve hot with whatever sauces or spices you'd like.
