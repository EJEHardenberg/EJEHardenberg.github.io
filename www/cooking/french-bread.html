Honeyed French Bread

Difficulty: easy

An Easy and Quick recipe for bread

Ingredients
  • 1.25 cups warm water
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon active yeast
  • 1.5 Tablespoon Oil
  • Honey (To taste)
  • Warm and wet towel
Directions
  1. Put a bowl of water (not your 1.25 cups warm water) in the microwave and turn it on
  2. Add yeast and sugar to warm water, let sit until the yeast is bubbly (about 5 minutes)
  3. Stir in the salt, oil, and flour
  4. Knead the dough until it's smooth
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  6. Cover the dough with your warm and wet towel, remove bowl from microwave and replace with covered bowl for 15 minutes
  7. Grease a pan and roll out the bread onto it, slashing the top and glazing with Honey
  8. Let it bake for 30-35 minutes and then enjoy it!
  9. I originally grabbed this recipe from this blog, and halved it and added honey. It's so good!
