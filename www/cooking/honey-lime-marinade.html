Honey Lime Marinade

Difficulty: Easy

Delicious and Quick Marinade

Ingredients
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 1 Lime or 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 3 tablespoons Soy Sauce
Directions
  1. cut garlic clove finely, place in a container. I use an old tomato sauce glass container
  2. Squeeze juice out of lime or pour lime juice into container
  3. pour olive oil into container
  4. pour honey into container, if you do the oil before the honey it won't stick!
  5. pour soy sauce into container
  6. Add Chicken, beef, vegitables or whatever you'd like to marinade
  7. Shake
  8. Store in fridge for 4 hours or longer
  9. Cook anyway you like! I like to poach my chicken in it's own marinade but you're free to do what you want!
