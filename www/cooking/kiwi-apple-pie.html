Apple Kiwi Pie

Difficulty: Medium

Tasty and Sweet pie

Ingredients
  • 1/3 Cup Sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2.5 tablespoon flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamin
  • 1/4 cup nutmeg
  • 5 Sliced Kiwis
  • 3 Sliced Apples
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 Pie Crust
  • stick of butter
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Mix sugars, flour, cinnamin, and nutmeg in a bowl
  3. Add kiwis, apples, and lemon juice to bowl
  4. Toss and mix bowl and let stand for 15 minutes
  5. While waiting, roll out and line pie pan with crust
  6. Use finger and thumb to make artistic bumps along the edge
  7. Pour fruit mixture into pie crust
  8. Slice butter thinly and place evenly spaced on top of the fruit mixture
  9. Using and excess pie edge, squish and mix with a dash of brown sugar to make a sweet crumble
  10. sprinkle top of pie with crumble or seal with a second pie crust if you have one (slice to vent)
  11. Cover edge of pie with tin foil
  12. Bake pie in center of oven for 20 minutes, remove strip bake for 25-30 more
