Cinnamon Chicken Empanada

Difficulty: Medium

Cinnamin Tortilla and delicious Chicken

Ingredients
  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 1/8 Teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 Baking Powder
  • 3/4 teaspoons lard or butter
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamin (or more, to taste!)
  • 2 Chicken breast
  • 1 Chopped onion
  • Cumin/Garlic powder (optional)
Directions
  1. Chop the onion and place to one side
  2. Slice chicken into 1 inch thick strips
  3. Spice chicken with Cumin and garlic powder if using
  4. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  5. Add in lard/butter until flour looks like cornmeal
  6. Add water to flour and mix until elastic
  7. lightly flour countertop and knead dough into ball.
  8. Seperate dough into 6 balls and roll flat with rolling pin
  9. place chopped onions and chicken into rolled out dough and wrap, closing over chicken.
  10. preheat oven to 350 and cook chicken for 20 minutes
  11. Serve hot and tasty
