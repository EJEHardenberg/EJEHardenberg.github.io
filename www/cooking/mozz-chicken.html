Mozzerella Chicken

Difficulty: easy

A simple recipe that looks impressive when plated, and it's tasty!

Ingredients
  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • 1/2 Red Pepper
  • 1/2 Green Pepper
  • 1 Slice of Onion
  • Spices (basil & garlic for me)
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cut peppers into 1 inch strips, place on foil on a baking sheet
  3. Peel apart the slice of onion so you have the curved pieces seperated into arches, place to one side.
  4. Slice chicken breast in two (this recipe makes two pieces of Mozzerella Chicken)
  5. Cut part of the way through each chicken breast in the middle, spread chicken slightly open
  6. Stuff the chicken with cheese
  7. Spice the chicken to taste (you can also do this under the cheese if you want!)
  8. Place chicken on top of peppers, then lay the arches of onions over each chicken piece
  9. Cook for 20 minutes
  10. Serve with noodles or whatever your heart desires!
