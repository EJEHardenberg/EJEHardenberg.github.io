Baked Cheddar Chicken

Difficulty: Easy

Simple baked chicken recipe

Ingredients
  • Chicken Breast
  • Shredded cheese
  • Milk
  • Melted Butter
  • Breadcrumbs
Directions
  1. Oven to 400 degrees
  2. Dip chicken in milk, cheese, then breadcrumbs
  3. Place on pan, cover in foil
  4. Bake for 25 minutes
  5. Brush butter on and cook another 10 or until golden brown
  6. Serve with noodles, sauce, and spices to your liking
