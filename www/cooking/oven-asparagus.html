Oven Roasted Asparagus

Difficulty: Easy

Delicious as a snack food, or as a side with dinner

Ingredients
  • Asparagus
  • Olive Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cheese
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Lay asparagus down in glass pan or on a cookie sheet
  3. Drizzle Oil over asparagus, no need to drench them, a few capfuls is normally enough
  4. Dash Salt and Pepper (and any other spices) over asparagus
  5. Splash soy sauce over asparagus (again, a few capfuls)
  6. Sprinkle cheese over asparagus
  7. Place in Oven for 15 minutes
  8. Eat!
