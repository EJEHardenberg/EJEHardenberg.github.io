Vegi-stir fry

Difficulty: easy

A really simple bunch of vegis that can add color and taste to any dish!

Ingredients
  • 1/2 a Summer Squash
  • 1/4 a sliced tomato
  • 1/2 sliced red pepper
  • 1/2 sliced green pepper
  • 1/2 sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • Vegitable or Olive oil
Directions
  1. Pour 1 cup of water into a pot and start boiling it for the rice, when it begins to bubble add the rice and turn the heat to low
  2. Add oil to a frying pan and turn it to medium heat, letting it heat up while you chop the vegitables
  3. Add the vegitables to the hot oil and stir occasionally
  4. Keep stirring/mixing until the rice has cooked for at least 20 minutes, then strain the rice and add it to the vegitables
  5. mix it all up and add soy sauce or any seasonings you want
  6. Eat! For a heartier meal you can add pulled pork, chicken, or pepperoni to the pan as well!
