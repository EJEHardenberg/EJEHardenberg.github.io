Prison Pad Thai

Difficulty: Easy

Pad Thai made easy!

Ingredients
  • 1 pkg chicken ramen noodles
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 2 tsp hotsauce
Directions
  1. Prepare Ramen noodles as per directions on package
  2. Drain excess liquid
  3. Stir in peanut butter, hotsauce
  4. Add more as needed
  5. Garnish with more crushed peanuts on top w/parsely
  6. Bon Apetit, cellmate!
