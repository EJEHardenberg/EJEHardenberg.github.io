How to remove all your google search history

Google recently announced a new feature, the ability to download your entire search history. This is a giant leap into transparency and being able to see what the company collects about you personally. At the same time, it also gives one the ability to remove the information. While we'll never be sure about what kind of back ups are kept, the search history being removed, combined with opting out of interest ads, can help lower your unique online presence.

Here's how to do it:

First, go to history.google.com

Next, select the gear in the right corner

Then select the option to remove items

And remove everything from the beginning of time

You're done!
