How to Contact me

Since I'm a lover of interesting problems, I'm always open to giving out advice if someone contacts me with a problem. So feel free to use the form below to get in touch with me.

If you're interesting in employing me professionally you should reach out to me via the form, please include details on what type of technology you plan on using or already use and I'll get back to you as soon as I can.
